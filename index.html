Safe Work Habits & How to Report an Incident

Modern cyber awareness training to keep you and your organization secure

Safe Work Habits

  • Use Multi‑Factor Authentication (MFA)
  • Lock and update your devices
  • Use approved software and secure Wi‑Fi
  • Protect confidential information
  • Keep work and personal accounts separate

Identify Threats

  • Watch for suspicious emails or links
  • Do not open unknown attachments
  • Be careful of fake IT messages
  • When in doubt, report it

How to Report an Incident

  • Disconnect from Wi‑Fi immediately
  • Do not delete related emails or files
  • Contact IT / Service Desk
  • Inform your manager
  • Follow IT instructions
