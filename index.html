Label It Right!

Sensitivity labels help ensure information is handled, shared, and protected appropriately, reducing the risk of data breaches and unauthorized access.

The Four Sensitivity Labels:

  • Unclassified

    Information approved for public release with no additional safeguards required.

  • Low Sensitivity

    Information for internal staff and approved partners where unauthorized disclosure would cause minor harm.

  • Medium Sensitivity

    Information for a limited group where unauthorized disclosure could cause serious harm.

  • High Sensitivity

    Highly sensitive information for named individuals only, where disclosure could cause severe harm.
